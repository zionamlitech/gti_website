Students and Programs

EXPOLRE OUR COURSES

Fashion and Textile

The Fashion design Technology is a course designed to train students to style fabric into shapes of different sizes . Designing a material is our core mandate. We have beads, millinery(hat) styling,Garment design

The fabric batique is a course in the fashion department where students uniquely design fabric into textiles of colourful patterns

The aim of this course is to enhance students in the department of fashion to design fabrics into textiles of different shapes and colors.

At the end of this course students students are given projects to accomplish as their practical test which is Assessed by their instructors

Building and Construction

The Building Technology is a course designed to train students to build and design structures such as Houses,crafts design using technical drawing techniques . Practical Construction is our core mandate.

The program is essentially design to motivate students to have a hands on practical knowledge which prepare students to graduate with the skills of building and drawing stuture plans at the end of their careers in the institution.

The aim of this course is to enhance students in the department to be artisans in construction field.

At the end of this course students students are given projects to accomplish as their practical test which is Assessed by their instructors

Welding fabrication technology

The Welding fabrication Technology is also a course designed to train students to join metals into shapes House gates, metallic window flames, electroplating and many more . Plates designs are our core core mandate.

This program is essentially design to motivate students to have a hands on practical knowledge which prepare students to graduate with the skills of joing and design metals plates at the end of their careers in the institution.

This programe is aimed at training students in the department to be artisonary welders in construction firm.

At the end of this course students students are given projects to accomplish as their practical test which is Assessed by their instructors

Electricals Installation technology

Electricals Installation Technology is also a program designed to train students to install electricals components such as Household and industrial electricals installation, electronics testing and many more . Plates designs are our core core mandate.

This program is essentially design to motivate students to have a hands on practical knowledge which prepare students to graduate with the skills of industrial working skills and building and testing circuits, motor construction at the end of their careers in the institution.

This programe is aimed at training students in the department to be artisonary electricians in construction firm.

At the end of this course students students are given projects to accomplish as their practical test which is Assessed by their instructors
