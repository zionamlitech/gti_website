The regional director of CTEVT visits Gyedu Technical Institute

The regional director of Commission of Technical and Vocational Education Training(CTVET) Mr. Asare Bediako Seth popularly known as ABS on his regional tour visited Gyedu Technical Institute (G.T.I) on the 22nd August 2023. The purpose of his regional tour was to visit the technical schools in the Ahafo Region to introduce himself together with his district team members.
