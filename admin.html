ADMISSIONS

MINIMUM ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS

The following requirements are to be met in addition to other programme specific requirements

UNDERGRADUATE PROGRAMMES

Senior High School (SHS) Certificate Holders
  1. WASSCE certificate holders must have at least six (6) credit passes (A1-C6) including three (3) Core Subjects: English Language, Mathematics and Integrated Science or Social Studies, as well as three (3) relevant elective subjects with a total aggregate of 36 or better.
  2. SSSCE certificate holders must possess six (6) credit passes or better (A-D) in three (3) Core Subjects: English Language, Mathematics and Integrated Science or Social Studies, as well as three (3) relevant elective subjects with a total aggregate of 36 or better.
  3. School Certificate/GCE Holders must have Five (5) credits at GCE “O” Level, including English Language and Mathematics as well as two (2) “A” Level passes other than General Paper in relevant subjects. They must also satisfy the Special Programme requirement(s) for programme(s) chosen

TVET Applicants

One of the following qualifications: Automotive Technology; Motor Vehicle Technician III (MVT III), Mechanical Technology; Mechanical Engineering Technician III (MET III), Construction Technology; Construction Technician Certificate III and Electricals/Electronics Technology; Electrical Technician Engineering.

POST-DIPLOMA APPLICANTS (Diploma Holders)

They must possess a Higher National Diploma or its equivalence in a relevant field of study from a recognised institution. (Successful Post-Diploma applicants may be placed at Level 200 or 300 of the four-year degree programme depending on their academic qualifications)

    POSTGRADUATE PROGRAMMES

    PhD. PROGRAMMES

    Applicant should possess:

    1. A researched Master’s Degree in the relevant field of study from an accredited institution.
    2. A cumulative Grade Point Average of 3.0 or above in the courses offered at the Master’s degree level.
    3. At least grade ‘B’ in the dessertation presented for the Master’s degree.

    M.PHIL/M.SC/M.ED/M.A/MBA PROGRAMMES

    Applicant must have:

    1. A good first degree (2nd Class Lower or better in the relevant discipline from a recognised University.
    2. A Pass in a selection interview and/or entrance examination to be organized by the Department.

      • Guide to use the four buttons
      • Use the first button (Add School) in case both your school and program are listed in the application.
      • Use the second button (Add Program Only) in case your school is listed but program is not listed. With this, you will be required to type the name of your program
      • Use the third button (Add School Only) in case your school is not listed but program is listed. With this, you will be required to type the name of your school.
      • Use the fourth button (Add School and Program) in case your school and program are not listed in the application system.
      • Guide to use the dropdown
      • Search through the dropdown by typing in a few characters of what you intend looking for. This will narrow the result set and thereby aid you to identify the school or program you are looking for easily.
